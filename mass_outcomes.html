Massachusetts Outcome Analysis Exploration

Julie Ghekas

20 June, 2016

Outcome analysis is performed here for Massachusetts. The matching scheme that we selected used an unweighted glm to generate the propensity scores, full matching retaining 1/4 of the potential control counties, and a population-weighted balance assessment. The groups formed from this procedure will function as the two groups to be compared to assess if the outcomes are different. The full pool of potential comparisons, i.e.all counties not in Massachusetts, may be used at various points in time, but these are not the primary comparisons of interest.
